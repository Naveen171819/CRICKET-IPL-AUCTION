Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Cricket Auction Simulator ("the App") handles your information.

1. Information Collection

The App collects the following information to facilitate the multiplayer auction experience:

  • Username: To identify you in the auction room.
  • Password: To secure your account (stored as a hash).

2. Use of Information

The information is used solely to:

  • Create and manage your auction rooms.
  • Identify participants during live bidding.
  • Save your team progress and auction history.

3. Data Storage

Data is stored securely on our backend server hosted on Render. We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties.

4. Advertising

The current version of the App contains no advertisements and no third-party tracking scripts.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us through the repository or the Play Store contact email.
